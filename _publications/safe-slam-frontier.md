abstract: >
  This paper extends the safe SLAM exploration framework with an optimal path planning strategy for frontier point
  navigation. The approach balances exploration efficiency and robot safety by computing optimal trajectories towards
  frontier points while accounting for the robot's safety margins from obstacles. The method was validated on the
  Diablo robot platform in complex indoor environments.
